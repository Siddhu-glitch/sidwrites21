When Love Haunts: The Why Behind the Pain

Should I be the only one who feels this way,
Or does love make everyone feel like this?
Even when it breaks me, I want to stay—
Why am I like this? Why can't I just walk away?

I’ve met you more in my dreams than in reality,
Lived a thousand moments with you that never came true.
I try to forget your smile, erase it from my mind,
But it clings to me, like a ghost that refuses to leave.

Why do I hold on when it hurts this much?
Why can't I just let go, when all you did was leave?
Why does your memory haunt every quiet moment,
Even though you’re no longer here to share them?

Why, why, why—
Is it love, or is it just me?
And why does it feel like I'm stuck loving the idea of you
More than the person you’ve become?
